About Us

Established in 1996, The Creative Coffee Cafe specializes in original blends of gourmet coffee and specialty desserts. At Creative Coffee Cafe you have the freedom to create your own unique blend of coffee! Do you enjoy a touch of caramel, chocolate or French Vanilla? Add it to your order! We offer a wide variety of coffee creamers, lighteners and sweeteners, and can accommodate all dietary needs. Do you prefer a dark, medium, or light roast? We have them all, and everything in between! Everything is customizable and you can even purchase a bag of fresh grounds so you can enjoy your personal blend at home. We also offer a large assortment of donuts and pastries that you can enjoy with your favourite coffee. All of our products are 100% fair trade.
